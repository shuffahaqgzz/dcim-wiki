# Risk Management Strategy

Risk management framework untuk DCIM platform.

## Risk Categories

### Technical Risks
- Technology maturity
- Integration complexity
- Performance issues
- Security vulnerabilities

### Operational Risks
- Data quality issues
- Process gaps
- Resource constraints
- Knowledge gaps

### Business Risks
- Scope creep
- Timeline delays
- Budget overruns
- Stakeholder alignment

## Risk Assessment

### Impact
- Critical: System failure, data loss
- High: Major degradation, security breach
- Medium: Minor issues, delays
- Low: Inconvenience, cosmetic issues

### Probability
- High: >70% likely
- Medium: 30-70% likely
- Low: <30% likely

### Risk Score = Impact × Probability

## Risk Response

### Avoid
- Eliminate the risk source
- Change approach

### Mitigate
- Reduce probability or impact
- Implement controls

### Transfer
- Share risk with vendor/partner
- Insurance

### Accept
- Acknowledge and monitor
- Contingency plan

## Risk Register
- Risk ID
- Description
- Category
- Impact/Probability
- Response plan
- Owner
- Status

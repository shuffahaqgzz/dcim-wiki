# ERP Solution Comparison

Perbandingan ERP solutions.

## SAP
- **Type**: Enterprise ERP
- **Strengths**: Comprehensive, industry standards, global
- **Weaknesses**: Very expensive, complex implementation
- **Best for**: Large enterprise, global operations

## Oracle
- **Type**: Enterprise ERP
- **Strengths**: Strong database integration, scalable
- **Weaknesses**: Complex, expensive
- **Best for**: Large enterprise, Oracle ecosystem

## Microsoft Dynamics 365
- **Type**: Cloud ERP
- **Strengths**: Microsoft integration, modular, cloud-native
- **Weaknesses**: Less mature than SAP/Oracle
- **Best for**: Medium-large enterprise, Microsoft ecosystem

## Odoo
- **Type**: Open-source ERP
- **Strengths**: Free, modular, customizable
- **Weaknesses**: Limited enterprise features, self-managed
- **Best for**: Small-medium enterprise, budget-constrained

## Comparison Matrix

| Feature | SAP | Oracle | Dynamics 365 | Odoo |
|---------|-----|--------|--------------|------|
| Cost | Very High | High | Medium | Free |
| Enterprise | Excellent | Excellent | Good | Limited |
| Customization | High | High | Medium | High |
| Integration | Good | Good | Good | Medium |
| Support | Excellent | Excellent | Good | Community |

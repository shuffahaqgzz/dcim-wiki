# SIEM Solution Comparison

Perbandingan SIEM solutions.

## Wazuh
- **Type**: Open-source SIEM + XDR
- **Strengths**: Free, good detection, active response
- **Weaknesses**: Complex setup, limited enterprise features
- **Best for**: Cost-effective, self-hosted

## Elastic SIEM
- **Type**: Commercial SIEM (part of Elastic)
- **Strengths**: Great search, scalable, good visualization
- **Weaknesses**: Expensive at scale, resource-intensive
- **Best for**: Large-scale, search-heavy environments

## Splunk Enterprise Security
- **Type**: Commercial SIEM
- **Strengths**: Powerful, enterprise features, good support
- **Weaknesses**: Very expensive, complex
- **Best for**: Enterprise, compliance-heavy

## IBM QRadar
- **Type**: Commercial SIEM
- **Strengths**: Strong correlation, compliance
- **Weaknesses**: Complex, expensive
- **Best for**: Enterprise, regulated industries

## Comparison Matrix

| Feature | Wazuh | Elastic | Splunk | QRadar |
|---------|-------|---------|--------|--------|
| Cost | Free | Medium | High | High |
| Ease of Setup | Medium | Medium | Complex | Complex |
| Scalability | Medium | High | High | High |
| Detection | Good | Good | Excellent | Excellent |
| Compliance | Basic | Good | Excellent | Excellent |

## Recommendation
- **Wazuh**: For cost-effective, self-hosted SIEM
- **Elastic**: For scalable, search-heavy environments
- **Splunk**: For enterprise, compliance-heavy (if budget allows)

# ITSM Solution Comparison

Perbandingan ITSM solutions.

## ServiceNow
- **Type**: Enterprise ITSM
- **Strengths**: Comprehensive, enterprise features, scalable
- **Weaknesses**: Expensive, complex implementation
- **Best for**: Large enterprise

## Jira Service Management
- **Type**: ITSM (Atlassian)
- **Strengths**: Developer-friendly, good for agile, reasonable cost
- **Weaknesses**: Limited enterprise features
- **Best for**: DevOps teams, small-medium enterprise

## Freshservice
- **Type**: Cloud ITSM
- **Strengths**: Easy to use, affordable, good support
- **Weaknesses**: Limited customization
- **Best for**: Small-medium enterprise

## osTicket
- **Type**: Open-source ITSM
- **Strengths**: Free, simple, customizable
- **Weaknesses**: Limited features, self-managed
- **Best for**: Small teams, budget-constrained

## Comparison Matrix

| Feature | ServiceNow | Jira SM | Freshservice | osTicket |
|---------|-----------|---------|--------------|----------|
| Cost | High | Medium | Low | Free |
| Enterprise | Excellent | Good | Good | Limited |
| Customization | High | Medium | Medium | High |
| Integration | Excellent | Good | Good | Limited |
| Support | Excellent | Good | Good | Community |

## Integration Points
- Ticket creation/update
- Status sync
- SLA tracking
- Knowledge base

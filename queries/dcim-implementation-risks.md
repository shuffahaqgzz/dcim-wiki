# DCIM Implementation Risks

Key risks dan mitigation strategies untuk DCIM implementation.

## Technical Risks

### Data Quality Issues
- **Risk**: Poor data quality undermines entire platform
- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: High
- **Mitigation**: [[data-quality-framework]], validation rules, monitoring

### Integration Complexity
- **Risk**: Complex integrations cause delays
- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: Adapter pattern, phased approach, proof of concept

### Performance Issues
- **Risk**: System doesn't meet performance requirements
- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: [[performance-requirements]], load testing, caching

### Security Vulnerabilities
- **Risk**: Security breach damages trust
- **Impact**: Critical
- **Probability**: Low
- **Mitigation**: [[security-architecture]], regular audits, penetration testing

## Operational Risks

### Knowledge Gaps
- **Risk**: Team lacks required skills
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Probability**: High
- **Mitigation**: [[training-documentation-strategy]], hiring, consulting

### Process Maturity
- **Risk**: Processes not mature enough
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: [[quality-gates]], process improvement, audits

### Resource Constraints
- **Risk**: Insufficient resources for implementation
- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: [[roadmap-strategy]], phased approach, prioritization

## Business Risks

### Scope Creep
- **Risk**: Project scope expands uncontrollably
- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: High
- **Mitigation**: [[roadmap-strategy]], strict scope management

### Stakeholder Alignment
- **Risk**: Stakeholders not aligned on priorities
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: [[communication-strategy]], regular reviews

### Budget Overruns
- **Risk**: Project exceeds budget
- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: [[cost-management-strategy]], regular tracking

## Risk Monitoring
- Monthly risk review
- Risk register updates
- Mitigation tracking
- Escalation process
